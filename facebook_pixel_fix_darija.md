# Kifash t-regler l-moshkil dyal "Traffic Permission" f Facebook Pixel

Salam, had l-message li kaytle3 f console:
> "unavailable on this website due to its traffic permission settings"

Kay3ni anna l-Pixel khdam, walakin Facebook **mabloki** l-data li katji mn had le site hit mazal ma3titihch l-permission.

### Step-by-step bash t-qadha (b Darija):

1.  **Dkhol l Facebook Business Manager** dyalk.
2.  Sir l **"Events Manager"** (Gestionnaire d'événements).
3.  F l-menu li 3la l-iser, khtar l-Pixel dyalk li raqm dyalo: **792412222997462**.
4.  F l-foq, klicki 3la **"Settings"** (Paramètres).
5.  Hbet l-teht ga3 htal l-partie dyal **"Traffic Permissions"** (Autorisations de trafic).
6.  Ghadi tlqa button smiytha **"Create Allow List"** awla **"Edit"**.
7.  Ktb le site dyalk: `faresmedicalcenter.com`
8.  Dir **"Next"** wla **"Confirm"**.

### Résultat:
Mili ddir hadchi, Facebook ghadi ywlili yqbel l-events (les visites, les clics...) li kayjiw mn site dyalk bla 7ta moshkil.
